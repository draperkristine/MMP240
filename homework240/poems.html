Running

I keep running But what am i running from? I have been running for so long I have forgotten why I slip slide and trip as I run I gain more and more speed it is like I am running down a steep hill Then I trip and fall the rest of the way down As I fall down the hill I hit every stone pebble and tree root Finally after what seems life forever I hit solid ground I pick myself up and dust off my clothes I look around my surroundings and see a clear paved road So this time instead of running I walk

Hope

Even in the darkest of times i still hope I hope because no one else will I hope for better days and happier times Where people are friendly and there is no more demise I hope because if I lose hope I lose faith If I lose faith then I lose love, self of being If I lose that then I will utterly become a ghost of myself Just an empty shell a memory of the past Destined to be forgotten by everyone That is why I hope Because even if it looks like the rain will never let up I see a beam of sunlight in the darken clouds

Why I Cry?

Why I cry I wonder to myself I cry for those who can't cry for themselves I cry for those who have no more tears to shed I cry when I see sadness in anothers eyes I cry because I can feel their pain I cry because I know how much it hurts I cry when I see the destruction of the forest Because I know it is in the way just a resource to use up I cry because of all the animals that have been killed Ecosystems that have been destroyed I cry because they never got a chance to speak I cry because the voices of lost souls have never been heard I cry because I dont know how to stop crying Don't know how to stop the pain the destruction the Agony That is why I cry

Pain

You ain't never felt pain unitl you been what I been through Beaten and batter beyond repair shatter like a broken mirror I watch as the shatter pieces of my life fall to the ground like pieces of a puzzle that may never be found Sometimes at night I look at the sky the darkness that surrounds the lumnious moon is like the darkness that is enclosing around my heart Sometimes I wish to be blown away with the wind let all my fear and pain drift away Sometimes I - sometimes I think with one click all the pain that I felt all the tears that I cried would finally disappear But I don't and I don't know why Espically when I know nobody would miss me So maybe I should just do it But every time I try something stops me I think I no longer want to live I say it every day but yet I still can't get myelf to put the cold heavy metal against my brain Even one day when the pain was so bad I cried and I cried I said good by to nobody and put the to my head Then I was given a reason to live instead A reason not to fly in the sky not to soar with the birds A little angel came into this world From the moment I layed eyes on the angel I knew I knew I would protect her from feeling pain from crying To say that the angel would never be afraid of this life I would be lying I would take every lick punch and kick I would make sure she is safe I would give my life to make sure she has one I will endure all the pain and tears I will cry silently so the little angel won't hear But know I am weak I feel tired So I look up into the sky and ask who will protect the little angel when I finally fly up and away with the birds in the sky
