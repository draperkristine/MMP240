A Lonely Ghost

I look around and time passes before my eyes I watch as my family expand and grow as the years pass me by The young get old and the old die Everybody changes everybody changes but me I watch the never ending cycle of life Watching it through the same eyes wearing the same clothes I don't change because I am a ghost Curse to spend my entire after life walking this earth I cannot haunt anybody because I cannot be seen or heard It gets very lonely here Sometimes I would go to 42st in Manhattan it used to be m down the many by favorite place with neon light and attractions As I walk down the many blocks people pass right through me At first it bothered me but as the years flew by me I got used to it Do you want to know the hardest thing to get used to? Being lonely it is my hell on earth As I walk down the blocks I peer through the store windows I see family and friends laughing and having a good time But more importantly they have each other where i have no one As I continue down the block crossing the street i think Why did i kill myself? Was my time in the physical human world really worth this hell? Maybe i am meant to be alone Maybe i deserve to become this lonely ghost
