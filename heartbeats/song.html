The Five HeartBeats

The Five Heartbeats is a movie based on the lives of the Dells, The Temptation, James Brown, Sammy Cooke and etc. It follows the careers of A vocal group called the Five Heartbeats. the film displays the rise and fall of a Motown inspired soul act through the eyes of one of the vocalist Donald 'Duck' Matthews.
